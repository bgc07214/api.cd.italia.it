Its extension extends to the northern Bay of Bengal. Therefore, weather over the country may remain dry with partly cloudy sky. However, from midnight to morning, moderate to dense fog may fall in some parts of the country’s river basins. Again, light to moderate fog may fall in some parts of the country. Therefore, inland waterways may be disrupted due to dense fog.<\/p>\n

From midnight to morning, moderate to dense fog may fall over the river basins of the country.
