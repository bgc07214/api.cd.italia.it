Recently, a new history has been created in the gold price in the country’s market while the price of gold is falling in the world market. However, the day after the record price was set, gold prices were reduced. The case of reducing the price the day after the price increase has never happened in the country before.<\/p>\n

Last January 18, the price of the best quality or 22 carat gold was increased by 1 thousand 400 taka to 1 lakh 12 thousand 441 taka. The price of a 21 carat gold bar has been increased by Tk 1,283 to Tk 1,7,309.<\/p>\n

Besides, the price of one 18 carat gold bar has been increased by 1 thousand 166 taka to 92 thousand 29 taka. And according to the traditional method, the price of one load of gold was increased by 933 taka to 76 thousand 632 taka. This is the highest price of gold in the market of the country. Gold was sold at this record price only on January 18.<\/p>\n

The next day i.e. January 19, the price of the best quality or 22 carat gold was reduced by Tk 1,750 to Tk 1,10,691. The price of a 21 carat gold bar has been reduced from Tk 1,633 to Tk 1,5,676.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
